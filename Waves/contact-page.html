Frequently Asked Questions

What information should I prepare before our first meeting?

To make our first meeting as productive as possible, please prepare information about your brand, target audience, objectives, timeline, and budget. Any examples of campaigns you admire or previous marketing materials would also be helpful.

How long does a typical advertising campaign take to develop?

Campaign development timelines vary based on scope and complexity, but typically range from 4-12 weeks from initial concept to launch. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our project planning phase.

Do you work with clients outside of your office locations?

Absolutely! We work with clients globally through our remote collaboration tools and virtual meeting platforms. Our team can travel to key meetings when necessary.

How do you measure the success of an advertising campaign?

We establish clear KPIs aligned with your business objectives at the start of each project. Our analytics team provides comprehensive reporting on metrics like engagement, conversion, brand awareness, and ROI throughout the campaign lifecycle.
